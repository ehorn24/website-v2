<About Drink Genius />

Anyone can be a top-notch mixologist with this app. Users can search for drinks based on name and ingredient and see detailed recipes, along with other similar recipes based on the drink they are viewing. The app can also suggest drinks for the user based on 3 of their preferences - type of drink, type of alcohol, and what flavor they like.
